Privacy Policy

Last updated: 25 April 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Moonstream Tech ("we", "us", "our") handles information in connection with our mobile applications, including Last Tile (the "App"). By using the App, you agree to the terms described below.

Information We Collect

The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information about you. Specifically:

  • We do not require you to create an account or sign in.
  • We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, or any other personally identifiable information.
  • We do not access your contacts, photos, location, microphone, or camera.
  • The App does not include third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking software development kits (SDKs).

Data Stored on Your Device

The App saves your game progress (such as current score, board state, and preferences) locally on your device using Android's standard storage mechanisms (SharedPreferences). This data:

  • Stays on your device.
  • Is not transmitted to us or any third party.
  • Is removed when you uninstall the App or clear its data through your device settings.

Internet Access

The App is fully offline and does not require an internet connection to play. It does not communicate with any server operated by us or by third parties.

Children's Privacy

The App is suitable for general audiences. Because we do not collect any personal information from any user, we do not knowingly collect information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time, for example if we add new features such as advertising or online leaderboards. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date above and, where appropriate, notify users through the App. Continued use of the App after changes become effective constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
