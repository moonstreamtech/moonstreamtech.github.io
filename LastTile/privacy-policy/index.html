Privacy Policy

How Last Tile handles your information.

Last updated: 25 April 2026

Last Tile is an offline single-player puzzle game for Android, published by Moonstream Tech. This policy describes how we handle user information.

Data we collect

None. Last Tile does not collect any personal data. No analytics, no tracking, no advertising identifiers. The app does not transmit any data off your device.

Data stored on your device

The following game state is stored locally on your device using Android SharedPreferences, and never leaves the device:

  • Current run state — board layout, score, turn count, combo multiplier, unlocked frame cells.
  • On-device leaderboard — your best ten runs (score, turn count, board size, timestamp).
  • Local settings.

You can delete all this data at any time by uninstalling the app or clearing app data through your device settings.

Permissions

The app does not request any runtime permissions and does not use the internet, location, microphone, camera, contacts, or storage.

Third-party services

Last Tile currently uses no third-party SDKs, no analytics providers, and no advertising networks. If a future version adds any third-party service, this policy and the in-app data safety disclosure will be updated before that version is published.

Children

Last Tile is suitable for all ages 7 and above. There is no user-generated content, no chat, no purchases, and no ads. We do not knowingly collect any information from children, because we do not collect any information at all.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy when the app gains new features (for example if an online leaderboard or advertising is introduced). When we do, the "Last updated" date at the top will change, and the new policy will take effect for new versions of the app.
