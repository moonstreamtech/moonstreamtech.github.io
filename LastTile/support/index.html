Frequently asked questions

Does Last Tile require an internet connection?

No. Last Tile is fully offline. All gameplay, progress, and the leaderboard work without an internet connection.

Will my progress be saved if I close the app?

Yes. Your current run is automatically saved to your device after every turn. When you reopen the app, you'll resume on the same board.

Where is my high score stored?

Your top ten runs are stored locally on your device. There is no online leaderboard at this time.

What happens to my data if I uninstall the app?

All local data — current run, leaderboard, settings — is removed when you uninstall the app or clear its data. Because nothing is stored on our servers, there is no separate cleanup needed.

Does the app collect any personal data?

No. See our Privacy Policy for the full details.

How do I request a refund?

Last Tile is currently free to download. If you ever make a purchase through Google Play and need a refund, please follow Google's refund process.

I have a feature suggestion. Where do I send it?

Send it to moonstreamtech@gmail.com with the subject line "Feature Request". We read every message.
