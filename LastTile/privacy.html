Last Tile is an offline single-player puzzle game for Android.

Data we collect

None. Last Tile does not collect any personal data and does not transmit any data off your device.

Data stored on your device

The following game state is stored locally on your device using Android SharedPreferences and never leaves the device:

  • Current run state (board, score, turn, combo, unlocked frame cells).
  • On-device leaderboard (your best ten runs: score, turn count, board size, timestamp).
  • Settings.

You can delete this data at any time by uninstalling the app.

Third-party services

Last Tile currently uses no third-party SDKs and no analytics or advertising providers. If a future version adds an ad network, analytics, or any other third-party service, this policy and the in-app data safety disclosure will be updated before that version is published.

Children

The game is family-friendly, has no user-generated content, no chat, no purchases, and no ads in this version. It is suitable for all ages 7 and above.
