Frequently Asked Questions

Does Last Tile work offline?

Yes. Last Tile is a fully offline, single-player game. It does not require an internet connection to play, and your progress is saved locally on your device.

Will my progress be lost if I uninstall the app?

Yes. Because game progress is stored locally on your device, uninstalling the app or clearing its data will remove your saved progress.

Does Last Tile collect any personal data?

No. We do not collect any personal information. For full details, please see our Privacy Policy.

How do I request a refund?

Last Tile is currently free to download. If you ever make a purchase through Google Play and need a refund, please follow Google's refund process.

I have a feature suggestion. Where can I send it?

Please email us at moonstreamtech@gmail.com with the subject line "Feature Request". We read every message.
