About the game

Mozai is a pixel-art coloring game built around discovery. There are no numbers on the cells — you pick a color from the palette, and the picture tells you where it belongs. Correct cells fill in instantly; wrong taps are simply ignored, so the picture always comes out right.

Pictures are grouped into numbered rooms. Finish the pictures in a room to unlock the next one. It is designed to be calm and focused — no timers, no streaks, no required accounts. Free to play, supported by optional rewarded ads.

Core features

  • Color by discovery. No numbers shown — select a color and find where it belongs. Correct cells fill; wrong taps are simply ignored, so the picture always comes out right.
  • Rooms that open up. Pictures are grouped into numbered rooms; finishing pictures unlocks the next room.
  • Pinch to zoom, drag to paint. Paint single cells or drag along a path; pinch-zoom and pan on large pictures.
  • Optional hints. Stuck on a color? Watch an optional rewarded ad to reveal where that color goes.
  • Offline-first. No account required; your progress is saved on your device.
  • Free to play with optional rewarded ads.

How to play

  1. Pick a color from the palette at the bottom of the screen.
  2. Tap on a matching cell, or drag along a path to paint several cells at once. Wrong taps do nothing — the picture can't go wrong.
  3. When every cell of a color is filled, that color leaves the palette so you always know what's left.
  4. Complete every cell to finish the picture, then unlock new pictures and new rooms as you go.
