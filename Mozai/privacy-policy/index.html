Privacy Policy

How Mozai handles your information.

Last updated: 27 May 2026

Mozai is a single-player pixel-art coloring game for Android, published by Moonstream Tech. This page describes how the game handles your information. For the studio-wide policy that applies to all Moonstream Tech apps, see the Moonstream Tech Privacy Policy.

Information we collect (locally on your device)

Mozai saves the following information locally on your device using Android SharedPreferences. This data does not leave your device and is not transmitted to Moonstream Tech:

  • Picture progress — which cells you've filled in for each picture you've started.
  • Room progress — which rooms and pictures you've unlocked and completed.
  • App settings.

Information we collect (sent to our servers)

Moonstream Tech does not collect any personal information through Mozai directly. There is no account, no sign-in, and no profile. We do not collect or transmit your name, email, phone number, location, contacts, or any other personal information. We do not track you across other apps or websites.

Third-party services we use

Google AdMob (ads)

Mozai shows two ad formats served by Google AdMob:

  • An anchored adaptive banner at the bottom of the screen.
  • An optional rewarded video that you can choose to watch in exchange for a hint that reveals where the currently selected color goes on the picture.

When ads are loaded or shown, AdMob may collect device identifiers (such as your advertising ID), IP address, and interaction signals to deliver and measure ads. AdMob's use of this data is governed by Google's Privacy Policy. You can reset your advertising ID or opt out of personalized ads in your Android device settings (Settings → Privacy → Ads).

Permissions we request

  • INTERNET — required to load ads.
  • ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE — used to check whether the device has connectivity, so the game can fall back gracefully when offline.

Mozai does not request access to your camera, microphone, contacts, photos or location. There are no runtime permission prompts during normal play.

Data retention and deletion (local data)

Locally stored Mozai data can be deleted at any time by uninstalling the app or by clearing its data through Android Settings → Apps → Mozai → Storage.

Data held by Google through AdMob is governed by Google's policies and controls.

Children's privacy

Mozai is suitable for general audiences. There is no chat, no user-generated content, and no in-app purchases at this time. Moonstream Tech does not knowingly collect personal information from children. AdMob is a general-audience service governed by Google. Parents who want to limit ad personalisation should use Android's family account controls and the device-level ad settings.

Your choices

  • Disable personalized ads in your Android device settings.
  • Watch rewarded video ads only when you choose to — they are never auto-played.
  • Uninstall the app or clear its data to remove all locally stored game data.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy when the game gains new features. The "Last updated" date at the top will change accordingly, and the in-app data safety disclosure on Google Play will be kept in sync.
